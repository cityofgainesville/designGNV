LET'S DESIGN
SOMETHING TOGETHER


DesignGNV is a community open to all neighbors who want to transform Gainesville! Together we explore, design and improve our local government and neighborhoods.


We aspire to be a diverse community represented by all race, nationality, gender, ability, ideology and skills. Join us!

Open Data Portal


About the Project

How can we simplify our community data and open it up for everyone? StatGNV lets you explore the City of Gainesville's data through interactive charts, graphs, and maps. You can now easily understand complex city data and track important trends in areas such as public safety, basic city services, human services, economic development, and others.


How to Participate

Check out statGNV and let us know what we should improve!


Explore statGNV
