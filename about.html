About designGNV




designGNV is an initiative by the City of Gainesvilleâ€™s Neighbor Centered Design team. We work on process improvement projects through the lens of human-centered design. Our goal is to improve how our neighbors experience city services and programs. In order to do that, we invite our community into the design process.

There are many different kinds of activities you may be asked to participate in depending on the project. We do interviews, focus groups, surveys, testing, and many more. Your participation helps us figure out the best way to fix problems.

Our Theory of Change

We exist to enable potential by pioneering transformation







Effectiveness

We exist to foster prosperity by lending support to imapct society for the better.

Efficiency

We exist to reduce friction by creating relief to impact society for the better.

Satisfactory

We exist to kindle happiness by nurturing inclusion to impact society for the better.







How does this impact the people we serve?






Individual

Design transparent, just-in-time services that are meaningful and impactful to the everyday life of a person living in Gainesville.

Group

If we look at Maslow's hierarch of needs - we need to work on the basics first - the core needs (physiologocal, safety). If our community stops struggling with these, we can work on a higher echelon - belonging.

Society

When we free ourselves of struggling through the transactional business of living, we start to imagine a society where people are empowered, creative m fulfilled and connected.







Our Methodology







Understand






We engage in research to understand the needs of our users and the ecosystem in which they operate.






Design






We explore new ideas that can help address the problem and design prototypes to test them out.






Test






We test these prototypes with our users. This feedback helps us arrive at an effective, efficient and satisfactory design solution.
